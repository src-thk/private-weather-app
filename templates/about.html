No tracking. No logs. No fingerprinting.

WeatherUI only serves you the weather data of location you specify manually. We don't even keep logs of location searched.

WeatherUI keeps your privacy first. Get only what you request.

This app is built using flask. A python based micro web framework. The API's used in this app send only two arguments (one of which is optional) which is what you provide manually in the form. We do not store or log your IP, location at you're or locations you searched for.
